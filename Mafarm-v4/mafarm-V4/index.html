Welcome to Manappuram Agro Farms Ltd. (MAFARM)

Manappuram Agro Farms Ltd. (MAFARM) is a professionally managed and diversified enterprise with a strong presence across organized retail supermarkets, agro-based production, and food service operations. Guided by operational excellence and a customer-centric approach, MAFARM consistently delivers high-quality products and dependable services across its business verticals.

Integrated Agricultural Operations

MAFARM practices sustainable, organized agriculture with a focus on quality, responsible resource use, and traceability—forming the foundation for consistent and reliable sourcing.

Managing Organized Retail Supermarkets

MAFARM operates modern supermarkets offering quality food and essentials, ensuring freshness, hygiene, and a reliable shopping experience through efficient supply chains.

Food Production & Service Operations

MAFARM operates production and food service units with strict quality standards, ensuring consistent products and a reliable customer experience.

Our Businesses

MAFARM is a diversified enterprise built on a strong foundation of sustainable agriculture, organized retail, and quality-driven production and food services. Through an integrated value chain and a focus on consistency, efficiency, and customer satisfaction, the company delivers reliable products and experiences across every touchpoint.

Farm & Agricultural Operations

MAFARM’s foundation is built on sustainable, organized farming focused on quality, traceability, and responsible resource management.

Supermarkets

MAFARM operates organized supermarkets offering quality food and essentials, with a focus on freshness, hygiene, and efficient supply chains.

Production Units

MAFARM’s production units follow standardized processes and strict quality controls, ensuring consistency, efficiency, and reliable supply.

Restaurant & Food Services

MAFARM’s food services use standardized recipes and quality sourcing to ensure consistency, taste, and customer satisfaction.
