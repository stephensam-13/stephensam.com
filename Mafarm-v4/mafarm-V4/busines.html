Manappuram Agro Farms Limited (MAFARM) is a public limited company headquartered in Kerala, India. As part of the esteemed Manappuram Group, MAFARM represents a progressive initiative focused on delivering quality food and beverage solutions at accessible prices.

Farm & Agricultural Operations

MAFARM’s foundation lies in its agricultural operations, where the company is engaged in organized and sustainable farming practices. With a strong focus on quality, traceability, and responsible resource management, these operations form the backbone of the company’s integrated value chain.

Supermarkets

MAFARM operates organized retail supermarkets offering a wide range of high-quality food and essential products. The retail network emphasizes hygiene, freshness, efficient supply chains, and a superior customer experience.

Production Units

The company’s production units are equipped with standardized processes and stringent quality control systems. These facilities ensure consistency, operational efficiency, and reliable supply across all distribution channels

Restaurant & Food Services

MAFARM’s food service operations are driven by standardized recipes, quality sourcing, and customer-focused service delivery. The focus remains on consistency, taste, and customer satisfaction.

Operations & Presence

Structured for Scale. Designed for Control. Built for Growth.

MAFARM operates through a centralized administrative framework that enables unified control, operational transparency, and scalable execution across its diverse business verticals. This centralized model ensures that all operational units and retail outlets function within a standardized system, aligned with the company’s core principles of efficiency, quality, and governance.

At the heart of this structure is a well-integrated network of operational units, production facilities, and retail outlets, strategically aligned to support seamless coordination between sourcing, processing, distribution, and customer engagement. This integrated approach allows MAFARM to maintain consistency in quality while optimizing supply chain efficiency and cost management.
