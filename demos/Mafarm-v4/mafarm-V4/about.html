Manappuram Agro Farms Limited (MAFARM) is a public limited company headquartered in Kerala, India. As part of the esteemed Manappuram Group, MAFARM represents a progressive initiative focused on delivering quality food and beverage solutions at accessible prices.

Established in 2008, the company combines industry experience with a forward-looking approach, leveraging modern technologies to create a differentiated value proposition for its customers. MAFARM operates through a structured and process-driven framework supported by key functional departments including Operations, Accounts, IT, MIS, Purchase, and Audit—ensuring transparency, efficiency, and strong governance.

The organization is guided by an experienced Board of Directors and leadership team, committed to driving sustainable growth and operational excellence.
